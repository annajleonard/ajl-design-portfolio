New Student

Services

Package Design, Illustration

Overview

New Student is a vibrant, student-focused design project that celebrates the journey of starting fresh in a new academic environment. The project features playful, character-driven illustrations and a welcoming visual identity aimed at helping students feel at home and inspired. The design combines bold colors, friendly mascots, and educational messaging to create an engaging and supportive experience for new students and their families.

Visual Design

The New Student project uses playful, approachable illustrations and a bright color palette to create a sense of excitement and belonging. The visual identity is designed to be both fun and informative, making the transition to a new school environment less intimidating. Each element, from the mascot to the informational materials, is crafted to foster community and encourage students to embrace new opportunities with confidence.
